FriendChise exists to remove the friction that shows up when a franchise grows faster than its systems.

## Core problems

- Uneven task distribution burns out workers and leaves others underused.
- Poor training leaves new staff guessing instead of following a clear system.
- Knowledge stays trapped at single locations instead of spreading across the network.
- Inconsistent recipes create quality drift that customers notice.
- New franchisees can repeat avoidable mistakes from day one.
- Franchisors and franchisees trust proof, not top-down advice.
- Mass hiring without structure drives up launch costs and lowers success rates.
- Managers keep rebuilding the same schedule every week.
- Staff turnover erases the shortcuts and know-how that make a store run well.

## What that means

These problems create burnout, inconsistent quality, wasted time, and avoidable mistakes. FriendChise is built to make the better way visible, repeatable, and easy to pass from one location to the next.
