Improve consistency across your franchise operations by sharing wisdom, optimal tools, and effective visuals. FriendChise enables parent organizations to manage franchisees through centralized task management, scheduling, role-based access, and a shared tools hub—ensuring every location operates with the same standards and best practices.

Production deployment: [friendchise.app](https://friendchise.app)

## What it helps with

- Role-based task management across organizations
- Timetable planning and recurring operational workflows
- Shared tools and conversion utilities
- Structured collaboration between parent orgs and franchisee orgs

## Why it matters

Franchise teams often lose consistency as locations scale. FriendChise provides a single operational system so teams can align on standards, execution, and visibility.
